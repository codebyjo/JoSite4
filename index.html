About

Hello World!

My name is Joanne Dang and I am a front end web developer, project coordinator and town planner.

Over the past few years, I've lead an eclectic life, earning a post-graduate degree in Urban Planning and cultivating mad skills in web development.

While I've worked with planning and building codes for a number of years locally and internationally, I now write another entirely different type of code! By combining my diverse background, I develop functional and aesthetically pleasing projects for the digital world - from web applications, responsive websites, front-end development, Wordpress development, open source data, Geographic Information Systems and things in between.
